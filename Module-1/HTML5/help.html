Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

Navigate to the Events section, find your desired event, and click the "Register" button. Fill out the registration form with your details.

Can I cancel my registration?

Please contact the city council office at (555) 123-4567 to cancel your registration.

Are there age restrictions for events?

Age restrictions vary by event. Check the event description for specific requirements.

How do I find events near me?

Use the "Find Nearby Events" button to allow location access and see events closest to you.
